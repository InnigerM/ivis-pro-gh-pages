Conclusion

Now, to come to our conclusion, no football league IS “the best” league. Every league has its own flair and atmosphere. But if we have to pick a league which is at the top then most people would say it is the Spanish league because they won the most Champions League titles. But if you look closely then only Real Madrid and FC Barcelona won some Champions League trophies over time. But if you look closely 3 teams each from Italy and Germany won the Champions League too. And 5 different teams from England won the Champions League too. With that, the more different teams won the trophy the better should the league be. More teams with high level of expertise improve the intensity and make the league more exciting as every team may battle for the title.
