Champions League Wins after 1992

The European Cup (in German Europapokal der Landesmeister) has been renamed to the Champions League as it is called today. With the creation of this league even more clubs could qualify for this tournament and not only the champions of a domestic league. As it stands today, the Champions League begins with qualifying round and builds then 8 groups with 4 teams each. The best 2 teams qualify for the round of the last sixteen. Teams which finished 3rd in their group get qualified for the K.O. stage of the Europa League and the teams which finished bottom get ejected from any European competition for this season.
