Domestic Results

Apart from their international activities, the top four leagues also have their domestic championships. All of the leagues also have a national cup tournament played each year as well as a supercup trophy. England even has two cup competitions mainly differentiating in which league tier their starting. The supercup trophy is played in all of the leagues between the cup and league winner. England plays it between the FA Cup winner and the league winner.
